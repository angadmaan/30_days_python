## 📖 About

This repository is my day-by-day record of learning Python properly — not just watching tutorials, but typing every line, breaking things, fixing them, and shipping small working projects along the way.

The goal isn't just "learn Python." It's to build the scripting fluency I need for where I'm actually headed: **cybersecurity and DevOps** — automation, tooling, log parsing, and the kind of practical problem-solving that theory alone doesn't teach.

Every folder here represents a day of focused, hands-on work. No copy-pasted solutions — if it's in this repo, I typed it, ran it, and understood why it works.
